Background

Who is Abdul Hadi?

I'm a Data Analyst and Dashboard Designer with a passion for turning messy, complex data into clean, meaningful visuals that drive real business decisions.

My expertise centers on Google Looker Studio, Tableau, and Power BI — the trifecta of modern BI tools. I design dashboards that are not just accurate, but intuitive and beautiful enough that stakeholders actually want to use them.

Beyond dashboards, I bring a programming foundation in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) and SQL for data wrangling, extraction, and deeper analysis when the BI tool alone isn't enough.

I believe that data without clarity is just noise. My job is to eliminate the noise.
